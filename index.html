More About Us

About Us

Welcome to Shriniwas Cloth Center, our premier destination for men's clothing in Jalahalli Village. With two strategically located shops, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of high-quality menswear that caters to every style and occasion.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for fashion and a commitment to quality, Shriniwas Cloth Center has been serving the men of Jalahalli Village and beyond for over 23 years. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide stylish, affordable, and durable clothing options for men. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our core values remain unchanged.

Our Collection

At Shriniwas Cloth Center, we understand that every man has his unique style. That's why our collection includes a wide variety of apparel, from classic formal wear to trendy casual outfits. Whether you're looking for the perfect suit for a special occasion, comfortable everyday wear, or the latest in fashion trends, we have something to suit every taste and need.

  • Formal Wear: Suits, blazers, dress shirts, and trousers.
  • Casual Wear: Jeans, t-shirts, polos, and casual shirts.
  • Accessories: Ties, belts, wallets, and more to complete your look.
